# About

**Openzeka Teknoloji A.Ş.**, founded in 2016 in Ankara Bilkent Cyberpark, is the
official **Robotics (Embedded Compute) distributor for Türkiye and the MEA region**
and an **NVIDIA Elite Partner**.

## Mission and Expertise

Openzeka distributes NVIDIA **DGX/HGX** servers, data center and professional GPUs,
**Jetson** embedded systems, and workstations for AI and high-performance computing.
The company also develops **Cordatus AI**, its own platform for real-time video
analytics and AI inference solutions across edge and cloud environments.

With expertise in deep learning-based AI, digital twins, and digital transformation,
Openzeka is one of the region's leading embedded AI providers.

## Product and Solution Portfolio

- **Jetson embedded systems** — AGX Thor, AGX Orin, Orin NX/Nano, Xavier, TX2, Nano
- **Server solutions** — DGX, HGX, PCIe-based systems
- **Mini supercomputers** — DGX Spark, DGX Spark Bundle, DGX Spark Triple, ASUS Ascent GX10
- **Professional graphics cards** — RTX PRO Blackwell, RTX Ada Lovelace (desktop & server)
- **Workstations** — Blackwell/Ada Lovelace GPU powered
- **Networking** — Ethernet, InfiniBand, DPU, Interconnect
- **Software** — Cordatus AI (IVA), NVIDIA Omniverse, NVIDIA licenses

## Cordatus AI

**Cordatus AI**, developed by Openzeka, is an open-source IVA (Intelligent Video
Analytics) software that processes camera streams in real time to provide operational
and measurable data. It offers scalable inference infrastructure from edge devices
to the cloud. Details: [cordatus.ai](https://cordatus.ai/)
